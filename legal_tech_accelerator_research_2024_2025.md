## 1. LexisNexis Legal Tech Accelerator Investigation

### Status: CONFIRMED REAL BUT INACTIVE

**Program Details:**
- **Existed**: 2017-2020 (5 cohorts total)
- **Location**: Silicon Valley (Menlo Park) + Raleigh, NC
- **Duration**: 12 weeks
- **Funding**: No equity taken, no direct funding provided
- **Focus**: Early-stage legal tech startups

**Historical Program Structure:**
- Weekly mentorship with LexisNexis executives
- Education sessions on product development, marketing, sales
- Access to legal data and technology resources
- Connections to Stanford, NC State, VC networks

**Notable Alumni:**
- Courtroom5 (pro se litigation tools)
- Specifio (AI patent drafting)
- Digitory Legal (legal cost analytics)
- Contract Wrangler (contract analytics)

**Current Status**: 
- Last cohort was 2020 (virtual due to COVID)
- No evidence of programs since 2020
- Website still active but applications closed
